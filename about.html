Our Story

THE LUMINA ACADEMY for IT and Cyber Security is an online-based training academy founded by Miss Olorato Mothomedi. We provide professional training in Information Technology, Cybersecurity, and Network Administration. Our dream is to bring light to students, graduates, job seekers, and organizations seeking to upskill their workforce.

Our Mission

Bridge the IT skills gap by offering accessible, affordable, and practical training that leads to real employment and career advancement.

Social & Economic Impact

We empower individuals with digital skills, promote inclusion, reduce unemployment, and contribute to technological growth within society.
